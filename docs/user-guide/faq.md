# Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and answers about GeoDataHub ERM.

## General Questions

### What is GeoDataHub ERM?

GeoDataHub ERM is a platform for ordering, processing, and delivering satellite Earth Observation data.

### Who can use it?

Any authorized user in your organization with appropriate credentials.

### What browsers are supported?

Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions recommended).

## Orders

### How long does processing take?

Processing times vary depending on parameters and current system load. Typical orders take 1-3 business days.

### Can I cancel an order?

Orders can be cancelled before they enter processing. Contact support for cancellation of in-progress orders.

### What formats are available?

Our system supports GeoTIFF, NetCDF, HDF5, and custom formats. See documentation for details.

## Files & Deliverables

### How long are deliverables stored?

Deliverables are stored for 90 days by default. Contact your administrator to extend retention.

### Can I download deliverables multiple times?

Yes, you can download available deliverables as many times as needed during the retention period.

### What is the size limit for uploads?

Maximum file size is 2GB per file. Large uploads should use the API.

## Troubleshooting

### I can't log in

Check your credentials and ensure you have the correct role assigned. Contact your administrator.

### Order is stuck in processing

This can happen occasionally. Wait 24 hours, then contact support if it persists.

### Download link expired

Request a new download link from the deliverables section.

## Getting Help

For additional support:

- Contact your organization administrator
- Review relevant documentation sections
- Check the API documentation for technical queries
